For host:
	Console Commands:
		SaveWorld <name>
		examples: saveworld test_world ,  saveworld test_world.json
		description: this will save your world to server/saves/<name>.json

		...

For client:
	Controls:
		LMB = Destroy blocks
		RMB = Place blocks
		MMB + moving mouse = Move Camera
		W, A, S, D = Move Camera
		F, G = Change Floor
		R = New connection / Cancel connecting
		T = End connection / Move connection
		MouseWheelDown, MouseWheelUp = Change the speed of the camera
